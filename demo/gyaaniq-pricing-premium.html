🤖 How AI Usage Is Capped (Fair-Use)

1 "AI action" = one model call — a generated quiz, an answer explanation, a doubt/chat reply, or a weak-area report. Each plan includes a monthly allowance that is pooled across the whole institute, so quiet students cover the active ones and you rarely hit the cap. A 300-student Growth institute, for example, shares a pool of 300 × 75 = 22,500 AI actions / month.
Pooled, not per-headAllowance is added up institute-wide each month — no child is individually blocked while others have credit left.
Soft cap, never a shockAt 80% usage the admin gets an alert. Past 100%, you choose: auto pay-as-you-go, a top-up, or pause AI for the month.
Unused credits roll onceUp to 20% of an unused monthly pool carries into the next month — rewards steady use.
Your cost stays safeCaps + top-ups mean every AI action is pre-funded, so your model bill can never exceed what the institute has paid.
